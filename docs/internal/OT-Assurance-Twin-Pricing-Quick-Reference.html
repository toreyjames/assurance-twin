OT Assurance Twin – Pricing Quick Reference

Commercial Model: Time & Materials | Target Margin: 50–60%

1. Core Product: OT Assurance Twin Pilot

Always Available – Base Engagement

LOE: 30–50 consulting days

Deliverables: Verified canonical inventory, blind spots/orphans identification, multi-layered completeness analysis, assurance report, executive readout

Positioning: Foundational visibility and assurance layer – the baseline that unblocks other initiatives

2. Add-On Options (Prioritized by Market Opportunity)

Stage 1 – Always Available

STAGE 1 Regulatory & Assurance Focus

LOE: +5–10 consulting days | Multiplier: 2.5–2.8× (premium pricing for compliance value)

What It Is: Transforms the baseline into regulator-ready evidence packs. Maps findings to NERC CIP, IEC 62443, FDA, or other relevant standards. Creates compliance-ready documentation with validation scores, traceability, and justification narratives.

Why It Matters: Asset inventory accuracy is consistently cited as a top pain point in regulatory audits. Most organizations struggle to prove "how do we know" when regulators ask about asset completeness. This option directly addresses that gap.

When to Offer: Always. Regulatory compliance is a universal concern in OT environments. Even if not currently under audit, organizations need to be audit-ready.

Data Requirements: Uses same data as baseline (no additional sources needed). Focus is on analysis, mapping, and evidence packaging.

Stage 1.5 – Often Available (CISO/OT-Heavy Buyers)

STAGE 1.5 OT Cyber Risk Reduction

LOE: +5–10 consulting days | Multiplier: 2.5–2.8×

What It Is: Uses the canonical inventory to build a prioritized risk register. Identifies which blind spots and orphans pose the highest cyber risk. Creates remediation roadmap (90-day and 12-month views) with clear prioritization based on criticality, network exposure, and vulnerability data.

Why It Matters: CISOs and OT security teams are already spending heavily on OT security platforms (Claroty, Nozomi, Dragos), but struggle to prove coverage completeness and prioritize remediation. The Assurance Twin's blind spot detection and cross-verification directly addresses "what are we missing" and "where should we focus first."

When to Offer: For clients with active OT security programs, CISO-driven initiatives, or recent security incidents. Also valuable for organizations trying to justify additional OT security investments.

Data Requirements: Uses baseline data plus vulnerability/security findings (already part of standard baseline). May include network segmentation data if available.

Stage 2 – Where Data/Maturity Allows

STAGE 2 Preventive Maintenance Optimization

LOE: +5–10 consulting days | Multiplier: 2.5–2.8×

What It Is: Uses the canonical inventory + CMMS data to identify under-maintained critical assets and over-maintained low-impact assets. Creates risk-based PM segmentation (Tier 1: must have robust PM, Tier 2: optimized intervals, Tier 3: candidates for PM reduction). Produces maintenance optimization playbook for the pilot plant.

Why It Matters: Predictive/preventive maintenance is a multi-billion dollar market with strong growth. Unplanned downtime costs manufacturing $50B+ annually. Many organizations have PM programs but lack data-driven prioritization. This option helps maintenance teams focus effort where it matters most.

When to Offer: Where CMMS data is reasonably linked (tag ↔ asset) and client has active maintenance/reliability programs. Best as follow-on after proving value with Stage 1 options.

Data Requirements: Baseline + CMMS work order history with asset/tag linkage. Requires some data quality maturity.

STAGE 2 Turnaround Planning Intelligence

LOE: +5–10 consulting days | Multiplier: 2.5–2.8×

What It Is: Uses the canonical inventory to validate and prioritize turnaround scope. Identifies high-risk blind spots that should be addressed during TA. Creates risk-based scope prioritization and deferral intelligence (what can safely be deferred vs. what's critical). Defines post-TA data capture plan to improve the canon.

Why It Matters: Turnarounds are massive, infrequent, high-stakes events. Better scope validation and prioritization can significantly reduce TA costs and risk. Organizations are willing to pay for intelligence that helps optimize these critical windows.

When to Offer: When client has a turnaround/shutdown planned within 12–18 months. Timing-dependent but high-value when applicable.

Data Requirements: Baseline + planned TA work orders/history. Requires alignment with TA planning processes.

STAGE 2 Digital Twin Readiness

LOE: +3–5 consulting days | Multiplier: 2.5–2.8×

What It Is: Assesses data quality and completeness of the canonical inventory against digital twin requirements. Maps gaps (what's missing for DT foundation). Creates roadmap for what's needed (data, governance, integration) to progress from Assurance Twin to full digital twin.

Why It Matters: Digital twins are a strategic priority for many organizations, but lack of clean, complete asset data is consistently cited as the #1 barrier. The Assurance Twin provides that foundation, and this option explicitly maps the path forward.

When to Offer: For organizations with active or planned digital twin initiatives. Best positioned as strategic narrative layer on top of baseline + assurance, not always a standalone paid add-on.

Data Requirements: Baseline data. Focus is on assessment and roadmap, not additional data ingestion.

4. How to Calculate Pricing

Client Price = Internal Cost × Multiplier

Multiplier Guidelines:

  • Core pilot (base): 2.2–2.5× (targets 55–60% margin)
  • Add-on options: 2.5–2.8× (premium pricing for strategic value)
  • Strategic first clients: 2.0× minimum on base (40%+ margin acceptable for reference sites)
  • Multi-plant / recurring: Can use 2.0–2.2× (scale efficiencies improve margin)

Example Calculation:

Scenario: Initial plant pilot + Regulatory & Assurance Focus

Base: 40 days × 2.2× = $88X

Add-on: 7 days × 2.6× = $18.2X

Total: $106.2X (47 days total)

Gross Margin: ~55–58%

6. Client Positioning

For Client Proposals:

"Time & Materials engagement starting with OT Assurance Twin Pilot (30–50 consulting days) to establish your verified baseline. We then offer strategic add-on options based on your priorities: Regulatory & Assurance Focus (always available), OT Cyber Risk Reduction (for CISO-driven initiatives), and specialized options for maintenance optimization, turnaround planning, or digital twin readiness where applicable."

7. Key Assumptions

  • Client provides data exports in agreed format and timeline
  • Executive sponsor and core team identified and available
  • Data access approvals secured before engagement start
  • No major overlapping initiatives that block data access or scope
  • Standard plant complexity (not greenfield or major transformation in progress)
  • Stage 2 options require appropriate data quality/maturity (assess during scoping)
