OT Assurance Twin – Time & Materials Delivery Model

Objective: T&M commercial model for early clients, targeting 50–60% gross margin
Target Margin: 2.0–2.5× multiplier of internal fully loaded cost

1. Roles & Responsibilities

Role Responsibilities Typical Utilization
Engagement Lead / Senior Manager Client relationship, scope management, value narrative, executive readouts 10–30% of engagement
OT / Data Engineer Data ingestion, mapping, Assurance Twin execution, KPI development 50–100% during build windows
OT Cyber / Controls SME Risk interpretation, compliance mapping, recommendations 10–30% of engagement
BA / PMO (Optional) Coordination, workshops, deliverable management 20–40% of engagement

2. Effort Estimates by Phase (Per Plant, T&M)

Phase 0: Scoping & Data Onboarding

  • Engagement Lead: 1–3 days
  • OT/Data Engineer: 5–8 days
  • OT SME: 1–2 days
  • Total: 7–13 consulting days

Phase 1: Canonization & Analysis (First Run)

  • Engagement Lead: 2–4 days
  • OT/Data Engineer: 10–15 days
  • OT SME: 3–5 days
  • Total: 15–24 consulting days

Phase 2: Readout, Actions & Tuning

  • Engagement Lead: 2–3 days
  • OT/Data Engineer: 3–6 days
  • OT SME: 2–3 days
  • Total: 7–12 consulting days
Total First-Plant Envelope: ~30–50 consulting days (role-mixed)
Subsequent re-runs (same plant): 5–10 days per run

Commercial Model & Margin Guidance

1. Time & Materials Structure

  • Standard Deloitte T&M billing by role and day rate
  • Provide estimate ranges (e.g., 30–50 days for initial plant) with clear assumptions
  • Manage scope and utilization within estimated ranges to maintain margin targets

2. Margin Target Calculation

Target Multiplier: 2.0–2.5× internal fully loaded cost per consulting day

Resulting Margin: ~50–60%+ gross margin (assuming scope contained within 30–50 day envelope)

Scenario Internal Cost Bill Rate (2.2×) Gross Margin
30 days (efficient) $X × 30 $2.2X × 30 ~55%
40 days (typical) $X × 40 $2.2X × 40 ~55%
50 days (complex) $X × 50 $2.2X × 50 ~55%

3. Client Positioning

"T&M engagement with estimated range of 30–50 days for initial plant. Subsequent runs and additional plants benefit from re-use and lower marginal effort (5–10 days per re-run)."

4. Strategic First Client Considerations

For strategic first clients / reference sites:

  • May accept slightly lower margin on initial pilot in exchange for:
    • Reference site / case study
    • Access to multi-site rollout (where margins improve with scale)
    • Market validation
  • Still maintain minimum 40%+ margin even on strategic pilots

Engagement Accelerators & Execution Readiness

1. Engagement Accelerators

Plant Data Readiness Workshop (1–2 days per site)

Aligns on objectives, maps existing data sources, identifies data owners, and agrees SLAs for providing Engineering, OT discovery, CMMS, security, network, and ticketing data.

Data Intake Checklist & Ownership Map

Clear view of what data is needed, who owns it, and how quickly it can be provided (per source), reducing cycle time and ambiguity.

Iteration Runbook & Templates

Standardized export mappings, ingestion pipelines, and workshop/readout templates so subsequent runs are lighter-weight (5–10 days) and focus on insight and decision-making rather than data wrangling.

2. Execution Prerequisites (Large Org Perspective)

Prerequisite What We Need Why It Matters
Executive Sponsor OT leader / CISO / Plant GM with authority Air cover, decision-making, kick-off support
Core Team Named Engineering, OT cyber, network, CMMS, ticketing owners (0.1–0.2 FTE allocation) Real people, not "in theory" – prevents delays
Data Access Approvals Security/legal sign-off for exports from each system Prevents mid-engagement blockers
Success Criteria Defined Written agreement on what "good" looks like Prevents scope creep and misaligned expectations

3. Complex Environment Check

Before starting, run dependency scan for overlapping/planned initiatives:

  • Control system upgrades / DCS migrations
  • Major turnarounds / shutdowns
  • OT security tool deployments
  • CMDB / CMMS migrations
  • Network segmentation projects
  • Digital twin / data lake programs
  • Regulatory audits (NERC CIP, FDA, etc.)

Classify as: Enabler (align/reuse), Neutral (note it), or Blocker (delay/scope around it)

4. What We Need From Client

  • Sponsorship & Time: Senior sponsor (4–6 hours total), named working-level people
  • Data & Access: Specific exports from Engineering, OT, CMMS, Security, Network, Tickets
  • Openness to Findings: Agreement that findings may challenge existing tools/assumptions
  • Joint Ownership: Client team co-owns remediation/next steps
